DESIGN VIZ PRO

Design Viz Pro allows you to deep dive into design and visualisation, from planning till execution. If you want to give 3D form to a design in the field of architectural, engineering, or landscape designs before they are built, then this course is best suited for you. Some of the major areas of the course are Simulation, Interactive Design, Video Editing, and Real World Lighting, On successful completion of this course, you will be able to make a career in the animation industry, architecture & engineering companies and events production houses.

Course Duration: 168 HRS

COURSE CONTENT

  1. Photoshop for texturing
  2. 3D design visualisation
  3. Real world lighting simulation
  4. BIM (Building Information Modelling)
  5. 3D Walk Through
  6. Video Editing
  7. Interactive design
  8. Overview of architecture

Software** Covered:

  1. Adobe Photoshop
  2. Adobe Premiere
  3. Autodesk 3ds Max
  4. Sketchup
  5. Lumion
  6. V-Ray
  7. Unity

COMPOSITING PLUS

No film, TV or music video is complete without going through the process of compositing. At this stage, different visual elements are placed together to create one scene or one sequence. The short-term Compositing Plus course at MAAC prepares you to excel in areas like digital design, match moving, rotoscopy, and camera tracking.You will be trained to work on Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Fusion, Mocha, Silhouette, 3D Equalizer, and Nuke.

Course Duration: 172 HRS

COURSE CONTENT

  1. Digital design
  2. Layer-based compositing
  3. Node-based compositing
  4. Art of rotoscopy
  5. Match moving
  6. Camera tracking
  7. Title graphics
  8. Stereoscopic pipeline
  9. 3D camera projection
  10. Live action compositing

Software** Covered:

  1. Adobe Photoshop
  2. Adobe After Effects
  3. Fusion
  4. Mocha
  5. Silhouette
  6. 3D Equalizer
  7. Nuke

ADVANCE COMPOSITING

Advanced Compositing course helps you learn different software for making movies. The course content includes Rotoscopy, 3D camera projection, and Live action compositing. On successful completion, you can work as a production artist or a graphic designer or even as a game developer. The short term course at MAAC covers software like Nuke, Mocha, and Silhouette. This helps to sharpen your practical knowledge, which is very much essential in the media and entertainment industry.

Course Duration: 72 HRS

COURSE CONTENT

  1. Live action compositing
  2. Planar tracking
  3. Rotoscopy
  4. 3D camera projection
  5. Stereoscopic pipeline

Software** Covered:

  1. Nuke
  2. Mocha
  3. Silhouette
